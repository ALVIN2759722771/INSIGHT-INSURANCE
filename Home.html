Types of Home Insurance Coverage:

  • Dwelling Coverage: Covers the structure of your home in case of damage from covered perils.
  • Personal Property Coverage: Protects your belongings, such as furniture, electronics, and clothing.
  • Liability Coverage: Provides coverage for injuries or property damage caused by you or your family members.
  • Additional Living Expenses: Covers temporary living expenses if your home becomes uninhabitable due to a covered event.
